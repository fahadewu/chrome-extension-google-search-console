How it works

Three steps. No dashboards to dig through.

1

Sign in with Google

One-time, read-only consent through Google's official sign-in. We never see your password, and you can revoke access anytime.

2

It finds your site

Open the popup on any page and it auto-selects the matching Search Console property. Switch sites anytime from the dropdown.

3

Read it in a peek

Clicks, impressions, CTR, position, trends and top queries — in the popup, or docked in Chrome's side panel while you browse.

Why use it

Same data. None of the round trip.

Search Console Peek reads the same official Google Search Console data you'd see on the web — it just saves you the tab-switching. Check the site you're looking at in one click instead of opening Search Console, picking the property, and waiting for it to load.
