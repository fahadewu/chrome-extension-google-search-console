FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is Search Console Peek? expand_more

Search Console Peek is a Chrome extension that shows your Google Search Console performance — clicks, impressions, CTR, position, top queries and pages, trends and period-over-period comparisons — in a toolbar popup or Chrome's side panel, without opening Search Console.

Is Search Console Peek free? expand_more

Yes. Search Console Peek is free to install and use.

What data does it access, and is it safe? expand_more

It uses read-only access to your own Search Console properties through Google's official sign-in. It never sees your password, your data stays in Google, and you can revoke access at any time from your Google Account permissions.

Do I need a Google Search Console account? expand_more

Yes. You sign in with the Google account that has access to your Search Console properties, and the extension displays data for those properties.

How fresh is the data? expand_more

The extension shows the same data as Google Search Console, which Google typically finalizes a couple of days behind. Figures reflect what Google's API returns and may update as Google finalizes them.

Can I keep it open while I browse? expand_more

Yes. Open it as a toolbar popup, or dock it in Chrome's side panel so it stays visible alongside the page you're viewing.

Who builds Search Console Peek? expand_more

It is built by Algoramming, a custom software studio based in Dhaka, Bangladesh.
