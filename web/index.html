How it works

Three steps. No dashboards to dig through.

Install once, sign in with the Google account that owns your properties, and you're set.

1

Sign in with Google

Connect the Google account that has access to your Search Console properties. One-time, read-only consent.

2

It finds your site

Open the popup on any page and it auto-selects the matching property. Switch sites anytime from the dropdown.

3

Read the numbers

Clicks, impressions, CTR and position — with trends and top queries — in the popup or Chrome's side panel.

What you get

Everything you check first, in one place.

📈

Performance trend

Current-tab insights

Compare periods
